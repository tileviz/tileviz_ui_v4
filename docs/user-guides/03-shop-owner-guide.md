# Shop Owner Guide

Shop owners manage their shop's tile catalog, sales team, and inventory.

---

## Dashboard

The shop owner dashboard shows:
- **Shop Tiles** — tiles approved in your shop's catalog
- **Sales Team** — active sales persons count
- **Pending** — tile requests from sales persons awaiting your review

---

## Managing Sales Persons

Under "My Sales Team" in the Dashboard:
- View all sales persons in your shop
- See their email and Active/Inactive status
- Add new sales persons via Admin panel

---

## Tile Catalog Requests

When a sales person requests a tile be added to the catalog, you see it under "Pending Tile Requests":

**For each request:**
- Tile name and category
- Who requested it
- **✓ Approve** — adds tile to your shop catalog
- **✕ Reject** — dismisses the request

---

## Inventory Management

Shop owners see all inventory items across their shop:
- Filter by room type and status
- Load any design into Visualizer
- Delete items no longer needed

**Statuses:**
| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Active | Visible and available |
| Draft | Work in progress |
| Archived | Hidden from regular view |

---

## Saving to Inventory

After creating a visualization:
1. Configure your room in Catalog/Visualizer
2. Tap Save → choose "Save to Inventory"
3. Give it a name
4. It appears in the Inventory Library for your whole team

---

## Notes

- Shop owners can only see their own shop's data
- Cannot see other shops' inventory or users
- Cannot access platform-wide Admin panel
